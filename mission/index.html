Call to action

Combating climate change

An analysis of outbreak frequency and severity over the last few centuries reveals that current bark beetle population spikes are unprecedented in their intensity, extensive range, and simultaneous occurrence in multiple ecosystems across North America.

Our commitment

Although mechanisms contributing to widespread outbreaks are complex and include spatial and temporal dependencies, climate change has been inextricably linked to broad-scale tree mortality, and will continue to push the historical resilience boundaries of some forest ecosystems.

We aim to mitigate these effects by empowering park rangers to more effectively diagnosis new or pending infestations, as well as by providing the tools to seamlessly analyze outbreaks on a broadscale.
